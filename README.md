# Corporate-Travel-Demand-Route-Intelligence-Dashboard
[Route & Travel Demand Intelligence Dashboard.pdf](attachment:1dae9b19-6b25-4c81-894a-878e30d62699:Route__Travel_Demand_Intelligence_Dashboard.pdf)

An interactive business intelligence dashboard designed to analyze corporate travel spending, demand patterns, and route distribution to support cost optimization and strategic travel planning.

---

## Objective

Provides clear visibility into corporate travel spending and demand patterns enabling organizations to monitor costs, identify high-traffic routes, evaluate departmental travel behavior, and support strategic budgeting decisions.

---

## 🛠 Tech Stack

| Tool | Purpose |
| --- | --- |
| 📊 Power BI Desktop | Data visualization & reporting |
| 📂 Power Query | Data cleaning & transformation |
| 🧠 DAX | Business metrics & calculated measures |
| 🧩 Data Modeling | Relationship building for cross-filtering insights |
| 📁 File Format | .pbix for development & reporting |

---

## 📂 Data Source

Corporate flight booking and travel records covering 2021–2025, including:

- Travel routes & destination cities
- Department-wise travel activity
- Ticket prices & total spend
- Travel class, airline & booking channel
- Monthly travel volume

---

## 🎯 Dashboard Goals

- Track total travel spend and trip volume
- Identify high-demand cities and travel hubs
- Reveal monthly and seasonal travel patterns
- Compare travel activity across departments
- Support budgeting and cost optimization

---

## 📊 Key Visuals

### 1. Executive KPI Panel

Total travel spend, number of trips, average ticket price, and route coverage overall performance at a glance.

### 2. Monthly Travel Trend *(Line Chart)*

Shows how travel demand shifts throughout the year, highlighting seasonal peaks and planning periods.

### 3. City-wise Travel Demand *(Bar Chart)*

Ranks cities by total trips to identify major travel hubs and high-volume routes.

### 4. Geographic Route Map

Visual overview of travel distribution across locations for route and network analysis.

### 5. Department-wise Travel Spend

Compares expenditure across organizational functions to surface key spending drivers.

### 6. Interactive Filters

Slice data by year, department, airline, booking channel, and travel class.
