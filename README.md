# Corporate Travel Demand & Route Intelligence Dashboard

An interactive Power BI dashboard analyzing corporate travel spending, demand patterns, and route distribution — built during my internship at TripTrip India to support cost optimization and strategic travel planning.

---

## 🎯 Objective

Organizations managing corporate travel across multiple departments and routes rarely have a clear picture of where spend is concentrated, which routes are overused, and how seasonal patterns affect booking behavior.

This dashboard provides visibility into travel spending and demand patterns — enabling cost monitoring, route analysis, departmental comparison, and strategic budgeting in one place.

---

## 🛠 Tech Stack

| Tool | Purpose |
|---|---|
| Power BI Desktop | Data visualization & reporting |
| Power Query | Data cleaning & transformation |
| DAX | Business metrics & calculated measures |
| Data Modeling | Relationship building for cross-filtering insights |
| File Format | `.pbix` |

---

## 📂 Data Source

Corporate flight booking and travel records covering 2021–2025:

- Travel routes & destination cities
- Department-wise travel activity
- Ticket prices & total spend
- Travel class, airline & booking channel
- Monthly travel volume

---

## 📊 Key Visuals

**1. Executive KPI Panel**  
Total travel spend · number of trips · average ticket price · route coverage — overall performance above the fold.

**2. Monthly Travel Trend** *(Line Chart)*  
Tracks how travel demand shifts throughout the year, surfacing seasonal peaks and low-activity planning windows.

**3. City-wise Travel Demand** *(Bar Chart)*  
Ranks cities by total trips to identify major travel hubs and high-volume routes.

**4. Geographic Route Map**  
Visual overview of travel distribution across locations for route and network analysis.

**5. Department-wise Travel Spend**  
Compares expenditure across organizational functions to surface key spending drivers.

**6. Interactive Filters**  
Slice by year, department, airline, booking channel, and travel class.
